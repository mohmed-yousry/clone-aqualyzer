We are working to make "Aqualyzer" an effective element in strengthening the national economy and achieving food security

Since 1989

We are Aqualyzer Company

We improve the sustainability of fish farming by increasing the efficiency of feed production and conversion to reduce environmental impact and preserve natural assets. The world is shifting towards improved agricultural practices and technologies to feed people sustainably. This massive increase has put food and water security With this in mind, we seek to expand our offerings across Africa and the Middle East as the region turns to local water sources and water wells to ensure food security. Technological innovation and internationalization have always been the driving force for our growth, enabling us to become well known in our target markets across the globe.

Our ambition is to contribute to meeting the growing nutritional needs of a growing global population in a sustainable manner. To be the number one provider of high quality fish feed that sustainably produces safe and healthy food
